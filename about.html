Computer Science Background

I started my software development journey in 2012, teaching myself web development in an attempt to recreate a childhood favorite video game (Fire Emblem: The Blazing Blade). Though I studied music and English literature in college, I'm continually driven to programming by a love of the activity of creating.

Since then I've developed my software skills continually on the job and at home, most recently rounding out my humanities education with community college courses like Data Structures and Algorithms and Mobile Development at LWTech.

I'm most interested in building applications and technology that either improve productivity, or are aimed at learning or improving a skill. And all the better if it can feel gamified and internally rewarding.
