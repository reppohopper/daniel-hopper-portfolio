Unity FPS Prototype

Role: Prototype lead · Tech: Unity (C#)

Overview

Small prototype focused on validating player feel and basic AI encounters. Includes modular player controller, simple cover-aware AI, and small test levels for tune iterations.

Technical Implementation

  • Player controller with configurable acceleration, friction, and head bob parameters.
  • AI using navigation meshes with behavior trees for simple state-driven actions (patrol, chase, search).
  • Level flow uses scriptable objects to configure spawn waves and objectives.

Challenges & Solutions

Tuning player feel across input devices—solved by exposing multiple parameters and logging telemetry during playtests to find balanced defaults.

Future Enhancements

  • Polish movement interpolation and add weapon recoil/haptics.
  • Integrate networked multiplayer stub for basic sync testing.
